## 🔥 What Is This?

LexAI is an AI-powered legal advisory assistant that gives instant plain-English legal guidance based on Indian law. Type your problem. Get advice. Don't go to jail.

## 💀 The Problem

- 70% of Indians cannot afford legal consultation
- Legal documents are written in a language only lawyers understand
- Justice delayed is justice denied — but also justice is just expensive

## ✨ Features

- 🤖 Powered by Google Gemini AI
- 📚 Covers 8 legal categories including Property, Employment, Criminal & more
- 🇮🇳 Indian law focused
- 💬 Plain English answers, no legal jargon
- ⚡ Instant responses — no appointment needed

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- Google Gemini API (the actual brain)
- Vibes

## 🚀 How To Run

1. Open `index.html`
2. Type your legal problem
3. Receive justice
